About Me

Hi again!!

Some time ago, I made a decision to pivot in my career and leave behind my other passion, animals, to focus on the world of design, art, and programming. Since then, I have been studying DAW and taking several courses related to programming and UI/UX design.

Nothing excites me more than seeing ideas come to life! I am an empathetic, creative and I love collaborating with my teammates. You will often find me brainstorming new ways to improve my projects and sketching out my ideas!

I believe that user experience is crucial in any digital project, and I am passionate about creating innovative solutions that are both visually appealing and easy to use. I am always learning and seeking out new ways to improve my work!

Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I look forward to the opportunity to work with you!
